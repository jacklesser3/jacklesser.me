## OPTIMAL ROSTER CONSTRUCTION

By the end, you should have:
- **Catchers:** 1 elite (Rutschman/Smith/Perez)
- **Corner IF:** 2 (1B/3B with power)
- **Middle IF:** 2 (SS/2B - get one early!)
- **OF:** 5 total (3 starters + 2 bench)
- **SP:** 7 (want at least 5 by round 12)
- **RP:** 2-3 closers
- **Bench:** Mix of upside and safety

## CATEGORY PRIORITIES (in order):
1. **HR** (2x weight) - Draft power early and often
2. **K/9** (2x weight) - Prioritize high-strikeout pitchers
3. **SB** - Get speed by round 7 or it's gone
4. **ERA/WHIP** - Comes with good pitchers
5. **QS/W** - Volume pitchers
6. **AVG/OPS** - Balance with high-AVG guy round 11-12

---

## KEY DRAFT RULES

1. **Round 1-2:** Best available hitter (power or speed elite)
2. **Round 3:** Lock your ace pitcher
3. **Round 4-6:** Balance hitting/pitching, fill C/SS
4. **Round 7:** Speed is GONE after this - get it here if needed
5. **Round 8-10:** Lock in SP3, SP4, maybe closer
6. **Round 11-13:** Balance AVG, get closers
7. **Round 14+:** Upside swings, don't play it safe
