2026 Post-Draft Report Card (Equal Category Ranks)

Each category is ranked evenly using roto points. No category weighting.
Method: For each category, teams are ranked 1-12 and awarded roto points (12 for best to 1 for worst, tie points averaged).
Categories: R, HR, RBI, SB, AVG, OPS, ERA, WHIP, K/9, QS, SV, W. ERA/WHIP are lower-is-better.
Total Score is the sum of all 12 category points (max 144).
